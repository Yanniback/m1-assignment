Old World Tarantulas:

Old world tarantulas are tarantulas from the eastern hemisphere including Africa and Australia. They are different from new worlds because they lack the urticating hairs that new worlds use as defense, because of this old world tarantulas have a more potent or commonly referred to as “medically significant venom” and because of this we usually do not recommend old world tarantulas as a beginner tarantula. They are usually faster as well and quicker to shoot a threat pose and even strike.
